# Privacy Policy

## Introduction

Welcome to Future Value Investment Calculator. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our financial planning platform.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information including:

- **Browser Information**: Your browser type, version, and language preferences
- **Device Information**: Your device type, operating system, and screen resolution
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, time spent on site, and interaction patterns
- **IP Address**: For analytics and security purposes (anonymized)

### Information You Provide

We may collect information you voluntarily provide:

- **Calculator Inputs**: Investment amounts, time horizons, and other financial parameters you enter
- **Preferences**: Currency selections and language preferences
- **Feedback**: Any feedback or communications you send us

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### Service Provision

- Provide accurate financial calculations and projections
- Remember your preferences across sessions
- Optimize the user experience

### Analytics and Improvement

- Understand how users interact with our platform
- Identify areas for improvement
- Develop new features based on usage patterns

### Security and Compliance

- Protect against fraud and abuse
- Ensure platform security and stability
- Comply with legal requirements

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

Most of your calculation data is stored locally in your browser and never transmitted to our servers. This includes:

- Investment calculation parameters
- Currency exchange rates (cached for performance)
- User interface preferences

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption**: All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to any server-side data
- **Regular Updates**: We keep our systems updated with the latest security patches

## Third-Party Services

Our platform may use the following third-party services:

### Analytics

We use privacy-focused analytics to understand platform usage without tracking individual users.

### Currency Exchange Rates

We fetch real-time exchange rates from reputable financial data providers. No personal information is shared with these services.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

### Access and Control

- **Local Data**: You can clear all locally stored data through your browser settings
- **Preferences**: You can change your language and currency preferences at any time
- **Data Export**: You can export your calculation results at any time

### Opt-Out Options

- **Analytics**: You can disable analytics tracking through your browser settings
- **Cookies**: You can disable cookies, though this may affect functionality

## Data Retention

### Local Data

Data stored in your browser remains until you clear it or it expires according to our cache policies.

### Server Data

We do not store personal financial information on our servers. Any usage analytics data is aggregated and anonymized.

## International Users

Our platform is accessible worldwide. If you're using our service from outside the United States, please be aware that information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
