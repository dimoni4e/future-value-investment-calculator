# Cookie Policy

## Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Future Value Investment Calculator ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on our platform. This policy should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, making your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

## Types of Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the platform to function properly:

**Local Storage for Calculations**

- Purpose: Store your investment calculations and preferences
- Duration: Until you clear your browser data
- Data: Investment amounts, time horizons, currency preferences

**Language Preferences**

- Purpose: Remember your selected language
- Duration: Persistent until changed
- Data: Language code (e.g., 'en', 'es', 'pl')

**Currency Cache**

- Purpose: Store exchange rates to improve performance
- Duration: 1 hour
- Data: Currency exchange rates and timestamps

### Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience but are not strictly necessary:

**Theme Preferences**

- Purpose: Remember your dark/light mode preference
- Duration: Persistent until changed
- Data: Theme setting ('dark' or 'light')

**Calculator State**

- Purpose: Maintain your calculation state during session
- Duration: Session only
- Data: Current input values and results

### Analytics Cookies (Optional)

We use privacy-focused analytics to understand platform usage:

**Usage Analytics**

- Purpose: Understand how users interact with our platform
- Duration: Varies by type
- Data: Page views, click patterns, session duration (anonymized)

## How We Use Cookies

### Performance Optimization

- Cache exchange rates to reduce API calls
- Store preferences to avoid repeated settings
- Maintain calculation state for better user experience

### Functionality

- Remember your language and currency preferences
- Preserve your theme selection
- Enable sharing of calculation results via URL

### Analytics and Improvement

- Understand which features are most useful
- Identify areas for improvement
- Monitor platform performance and reliability

## Managing Your Cookie Preferences

### Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

**Google Chrome**

1. Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
2. Choose your preferred cookie settings

**Mozilla Firefox**

1. Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
2. Manage your cookie preferences

**Safari**

1. Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
2. Control cookie storage and access

**Microsoft Edge**

1. Settings → Cookies and site permissions
2. Adjust cookie preferences

### Platform-Specific Controls

**Clear Calculation Data**
You can clear all stored calculation data by:

- Using the "Clear All" button in the calculator
- Clearing your browser's local storage
- Using browser developer tools

**Disable Analytics**
You can opt out of analytics by:

- Enabling "Do Not Track" in your browser
- Using privacy-focused browser extensions
- Disabling JavaScript (may affect functionality)
